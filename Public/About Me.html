About Me

I am a dedicated and proactive professional with a strong curiosity for learning and a commitment to continuous
improvement in my work. My creative passion for all art forms enriches my corporate endeavors, allowing me to bring
fresh perspectives and innovative solutions.

With a background in UX design, I have collaborated with organizations of various sizes, from large enterprises to
small startups. I am now seeking new challenges that will enable me to leverage my UX expertise in the development field.
My skills include creating design systems and style guides, fostering collaborative teamwork, and applying agile methodologies
such as Scrum and Kanban. I excel in problem-solving, possess strong communication and presentation skills, and have a keen
attention to detail.

I am proficient in various prototyping and design collaboration tools, including InVision, Azure, Miro, and Zeplin,
as well as project management tools like Trello and JIRA.

On a personal note, I am an easy-going individual who enjoys reading, writing, watching TV shows and movies, attending concerts,
and playing Dungeons & Dragons with friends. Despite being naturally shy, I strive to overcome my barriers and include others in my
endeavors, fostering a collaborative environment.
